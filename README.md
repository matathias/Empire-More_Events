# Empire Refactored: More Events

A straight-forward mod that adds many new events to [Empire Refactored](https://github.com/matathias/Empire-1_6-Continued), ranging from crises to pure boons; from rare and powerful events that target the entire Empire, to small and benign events that simply add some flavor to a playthrough.

With a larger pool of events to pull from, variety will feel greater than ever before. Most of them feature options with differing outcomes, allowing you to shape your empire as you see fit.

## Event List

**Abandoned Outpost** — Uncommon\
Scouts discover an intact but abandoned outpost near your settlement.

**Ancient Grove** — Uncommon | Forest biomes only\
A magnificent primeval forest with exceptional timber is discovered.

**Archotech Signal** — Very Rare | Ultra tech\
Every comms array in your empire activates simultaneously with an incomprehensible archotech transmission.

**Archaeological Discovery** — Uncommon\
Miners uncover ancient ruins beneath a settlement.

**Armory Fire** — Uncommon | Requires weapons production\
Fire breaks out in your settlement's armory, destroying stockpiles and equipment.

**Arms Dealer** — Common\
A weapons dealer arrives offering bulk military equipment at steep discounts.

**Border Skirmish** — Common\
Hostile forces mass near your border settlements.

**Bountiful Harvest Season** — Common | Requires food production\
An unusually fertile season produces abundant crops.

**Bountiful Trade Routes** — Uncommon | Requires 3+ settlements\
Trade routes are thriving across your territory, boosting tithe collection and trade revenue empire-wide.

**Brain Drain** — Common | Low happiness\
Skilled workers and researchers are leaving for greener pastures.

**Chemfuel Geyser** — Uncommon | Industrial tech, requires chemfuel production\
Workers strike a natural chemfuel seep.

**Chemfuel Spill** — Common | Industrial tech, requires chemfuel production\
A storage tank ruptures, spilling volatile fuel across the settlement.

**Corruption Scandal** — Common\
Local officials are caught skimming taxes.

**Counterfeiting Ring** — Uncommon\

**Crop Blight** — Common | Requires food production\
